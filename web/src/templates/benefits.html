Features & Benefits

Features

Benefits

1. Clear display of nutritional information in a table
  • For users who want to get nutritional information such as micro and macro nutrition for various reasons. These users can be athletes or any one who wants to follow a diet or needs to pay careful attention to their meals' nutritional information. This feature can save users a lot of time by automating what could take a much longer time just by searching by hand and calculating the nutritions.
2. Choosing alternate food options
  • For users who want to have user interaction with the classifier. Users can correct the classifier in case misclassification. For example between long and short grained white rice there is a higher uncertainty and users can fix that by choosing given options. This is important for users who are looking for more user input, interactions, and when they observe a mistake and want to easily correct it.
3. Addition and removal of each scanned item for a total nutitional info display.
  • For users who want to view the amount of nutritional info they will be consuming in a meal. This feature helps users to add meal or remove unwanted ones in case they change their mind and can view the total sum at the end. This is important for users who want to view an entire meals nutritional info and manage the items in the meal.
4. Saving and logging meals
  • Users who want to log their meals daily and follow a routine. This feature can help users to save and view their nutritional information per meal every day for an easier developement of a habbit. This is important for users who want to tract their meals and follow a daily pattern/goal.
5. General scale display options, weight, units, and tare.
  • For those who also want to use the scale. Users can see the weight of the food. They can change nutritional info units for different serving amounts or change item weight units grams, lbs, etc for easier use. The can also use tare to zero out the current weight. This is important for users who are also looking to use the scale separately when they want to.
