About Our Product

What is SnailScale?

A scale that displays food facts including calories, macro nutrients such as protien, fat, and carbs, and micro- nutrients like iron, soduim, etc. Based on the type of food or ingredient on it.

How does SnailScale do it?

SnailScale uses a camera to detect the item on it. once the item is detected, all dietary nutritions are calculated based on weight on the food, the units (g, oz, ...) doesn't matter!

Who SnailScale is for?

  • Professional Athletes who need to pay attention to their food intake and change their diet
  • Health & Fitness Enthusiasts who what to pay attention to macro nutrients
  • People with a strict diet such as Keto for weight gain/loss and need to watch for carb intake
  • Those with health conditions that requires them to consume a set amount of micro nutrients in their food. For example, Iron or Sodium.

Key Benefits:

  • SnailScale contains nutritional facts for alot of food items so there is no need to search them up online
  • The ingredient are detected and calculated automatically no need to calculate anyhting by hand or type in the food name into the device.
  • You can save alot of time for measuring and calculating the nutritions and spend that time on other things
