Key Product Interactions

Total nutritions

using add, remove, and sum buttons users can create a meal and view the total nutritional info of their current meal.

Total Meal info

Users can log the created meal and save it in a meal diary, they can come back to view their diary at anytime.

Scale Display, Tare and Units

Users can view item weight, use tare button to zero out the scale and change units as they see fit.

Nutritional Info Serving Size

Users can change nutriotinal info serving units based on their needs.
