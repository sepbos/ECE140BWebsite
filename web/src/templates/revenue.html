Revenue Model

Sources of revenue streams:

For our revenue sources we have decided to utilize a one time purchase of the product which is the scale and subscription based service that offers a detialed display of the nutritional information,as well as a list of saved meals that user can view at any time. Each unit price is $50 and subscription service is $2 per month.

Channels of Revenue:

We are planning to sell our physical product which includes the scale and camera on our website or other retail stores and websites such as BestBuy or Amazon. We offer the subscription based plan on our website or the app after the user signs up.

Annual revenue:

The expected annual revenue for each channel would inclue:
  • Sales: Total sale of 10,000 units for the first year after successful advertisement and marketing which be a total of $500,000.
  • Subscription: We expect an average user to use our subsription plan for up to 3 months and maybe to upwards of 6 months only because the subscription is very low cost of $2. Based on mentioned number of sales we expect minimum of $60,000 revenue from subscription services only.
